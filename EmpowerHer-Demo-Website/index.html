About EmpowerHER Program !!!

We at GirlScript are excited to partner with Masai School, along with KSIF (Korean Social Investment Foundation) and AVPN (Asian Venture Philanthropy Network), to bring you the EmpowerHER program. This initiative offers 100% scholarships for women, designed to help launch or relaunch tech careers through tailored courses, job placement assistance, internships, and continuous support.

Who Is This Program For?

  • Freshers: Gain job-ready skills.
  • Career Returners: Re-enter the workforce.
  • Tech Aspirants: Break into the tech industry.
